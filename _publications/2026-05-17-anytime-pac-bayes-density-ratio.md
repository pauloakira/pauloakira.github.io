A unified framework for learning under covariate shift: a constrained
density-ratio network approximates the Radon-Nikodym derivative and feeds an
*anytime* PAC-Bayes generalization certificate. Structural constraints are
enforced through an augmented-Lagrangian scheme, with validation on a
two-campaign protocol over both synthetic and real data.
